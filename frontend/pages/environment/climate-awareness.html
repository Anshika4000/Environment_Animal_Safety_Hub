Understanding Climate Change

Climate change isn't just about warmer weather. It's a complex system affecting every aspect of our planet's ecosystem.

Human Causes

The primary drivers of climate change stem from human activities:

  • Burning fossil fuels (coal, oil, natural gas)
  • Deforestation and land use changes
  • Industrial processes and manufacturing
  • Agricultural practices and livestock
  • Transportation emissions
  • Waste management systems

Global Impacts

Climate change affects every corner of our planet:

  • Rising global temperatures and heatwaves
  • Melting glaciers and polar ice caps
  • Sea level rise threatening coastal communities
  • Extreme weather events (storms, floods, droughts)
  • Ocean acidification harming marine life
  • Biodiversity loss and species extinction

About EcoLife

Caring for Animals & Nature

EcoLife is dedicated to protecting pets, wildlife, plants, and the planet through community action and education.

Our Mission

Creating a safer world for animals and a greener planet for future generations.

Pet Welfare

Rescuing, feeding, and caring for stray animals. Providing medical care and finding loving homes.

Plant Protection

Encouraging tree planting, green living, and protecting natural habitats from destruction.

Community Care

Connecting people to protect animals together through education and volunteer programs.

Why EcoLife?

We believe every animal deserves care and every plant deserves protection. Small actions lead to lasting impact. Since 2020, we've grown from a small group of volunteers to a nationwide movement.

Data Sources & Methodology

Understanding how we monitor and analyze soil moisture data

Soil Moisture Monitoring Technology

Our system uses advanced sensors and algorithms to provide accurate, real-time soil moisture monitoring. The technology combines:

  • Capacitive Sensors: Measure soil dielectric constant to determine moisture content
  • Tensiometers: Measure soil water potential and plant-available water
  • IoT Integration: Wireless data transmission for real-time monitoring
  • Weather Correlation: Integration with local weather data for accurate predictions
  • Machine Learning: Predictive algorithms for irrigation optimization

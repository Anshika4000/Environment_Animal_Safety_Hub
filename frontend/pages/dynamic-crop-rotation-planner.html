Data Sources & Methodology

Understanding the science behind our crop rotation recommendations

How We Calculate Rotation Plans

Our crop rotation planner uses a combination of scientific research, regional climate data, and soil science principles to generate optimal rotation sequences. The algorithm considers:

  • Nutrient Cycling: Crops have different nutrient requirements and contributions
  • Pest Management: Breaking pest and disease cycles through crop diversity
  • Soil Structure: Root systems that improve or maintain soil health
  • Climate Adaptation: Seasonal timing and weather considerations
  • Economic Factors: Market prices and input costs

Carbon Sequestration Methodology

Calculation Methods

Carbon sequestration rates are calculated using empirical models based on:

  • IPCC Guidelines: International standards for greenhouse gas inventories
  • USDA COMET-Farm: Comprehensive farm-level carbon accounting
  • Scientific Literature: Meta-analyses of field studies and long-term experiments

Key Assumptions

  • Soil organic carbon saturation occurs at 1-3% depending on soil type
  • Decomposition rates vary by climate zone and soil conditions
  • Practice effects are additive but may have interaction effects
  • Long-term sequestration rates decline as soil approaches saturation
